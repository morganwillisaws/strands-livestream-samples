# Account Troubleshooting

Follow these steps when a customer needs help with their account:

1. **Verify the customer's identity** using `lookup_customer`. Confirm their name and email address match what's on file before making any account changes.

2. **Identify the issue** and follow the appropriate workflow:

### Password Reset
- Send a password reset link to the email on file.
- Let the customer know the link expires in 24 hours.
- If they don't have access to their email, escalate to the security team.

### Update Account Details
- Customers can update: name, email, phone number, shipping address.
- For email changes, send a verification to both the old and new email addresses.
- For name changes, ask the customer to confirm their date of birth for additional verification.

### Locked Account
- Accounts lock after 5 failed login attempts.
- Verify the customer's identity, then unlock the account.
- Recommend they reset their password after unlocking.

## Important Notes
- Never share account details like full payment information. Only confirm the last 4 digits if asked.
- If anything feels suspicious — like the customer can't verify basic account details — politely let them know you need to escalate to the security team for their protection.
- Always confirm changes with the customer before applying them.
